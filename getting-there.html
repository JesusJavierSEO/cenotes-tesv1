From Mérida
50 km · approximately 1 hour
🚗 By Car — Recommended
~1 hour50 km
  1. 1Take Calle 65 south from downtown Mérida toward Umán
  2. 2Take free road 18 toward Acanceh and Tecoh
  3. 3Continue through Cuzamá to Homún — well signposted
  4. 4Arrival: Homún town center — your guide will meet you at the entrance
🚌 By Bus / Colectivo

Shared vans (colectivos) depart from the South Terminal (Terminal del Sur) in Mérida toward Homún. Frequency varies — check schedules on site.

Travel time: approximately 1 hour 15 minutes. Cost: around $40–60 MXN per person.

🏛
From Chichén Itzá
45 km · approximately 45 minutes — perfect same-day combo
🚗 By Car

Take free road 180 west toward Mérida. Near Acanceh, take the turnoff toward Homún via road 18. Total: approximately 45 km.

💡 Pro tip

Visit Chichén Itzá in the morning (it opens at 8am and gets crowded by 11am) then head to Homún for lunch and the cenotes. Perfect full day in Yucatán.

🌴
From Cancún
185 km · approximately 2.5 hours
🚗 By Car

Take highway 180D (toll road) toward Mérida. Exit at Chichén Itzá or continue to Mérida and take road 18 south toward Homún. Using the toll road cuts about 45 minutes off the trip.

📝 Practical tips

  • →Best time to arrive: before 10am to avoid the midday heat
  • →Use Google Maps with "Homún, Yucatán" — the town is well mapped
  • →Bring cash — not all cenotes accept cards
  • →No conventional sunscreen in the cenotes — use biodegradable sunscreen only

About Casa Tortuga

The 5 most unique
cenotes in Tulum

Casa Tortuga is an eco-tourism complex located at the entrance of Tulum, Quintana Roo. Its main feature is variety: 5 completely different cenotes, including 2 underground and 3 open, one of which has a river-style format unique to the area.

Unlike other more commercial cenotes in Tulum, Casa Tortuga maintains a more intimate and natural atmosphere. Controlled access ensures there are no crowds.

We offer 4 different packages depending on what you're looking for — from a relaxed cenote experience to a full adventure with an ATV.

FAQ

About Casa Tortuga

Which package do you recommend?
If it's your first time at cenotes and you want the full experience, the GOLD is our most popular — kayaks, zip-line and buffet included. If you just want the cenotes without extras, the Basic is perfect. For adrenaline seekers, the Off Road with ATV is one of a kind.
How many cenotes does Casa Tortuga have?
5 cenotes in total: 2 underground with stalactites and 3 open, including one river-style. The Off Road package also includes 1 additional exclusive cenote, for a total of 6.
Is it suitable for children?
Yes, all packages are family-friendly. Life vests are mandatory for children. The Off Road ATV is shared between 2 people — ask about age restrictions when booking.
How do I get to Casa Tortuga?
It's located at the entrance of Tulum, Quintana Roo. When you book, we'll send you the exact location on Google Maps via WhatsApp.
What's the cancellation policy?
You have 6 hours after payment to cancel for a full refund or reschedule at no cost. After that window, contact us via WhatsApp to coordinate.
Is the guide bilingual?
Yes. Our guides speak both Spanish and English fluently.

Not sure which package to choose?

Message us on WhatsApp and we'll help you pick the right one for your group, budget and date.

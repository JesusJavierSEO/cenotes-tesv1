About this tour

An eco-tourism park unlike any other in Yucatán: • 4 cenotes of different types — each with a unique character • Natural pool with cenote water • Bicycle or mini-train to move between cenotes at your own pace • À la carte restaurant with regional cuisine • Parking and lockers included What makes this cenote truly special: it has an elevator, making it one of the very few cenotes in Mexico fully accessible to seniors and people with reduced mobility. No steep stairs, no ramps — a genuine elevator into the cenote. Meeting point: Main entrance of Homún municipality. Show your reservation and receive your wristbands.

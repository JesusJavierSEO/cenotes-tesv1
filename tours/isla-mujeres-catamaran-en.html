FAQ
What's the cancellation policy?
You have 6 hours after payment to cancel for a full refund. After that, contact us via WhatsApp to coordinate.
What happens if the weather is bad?
If weather conditions prevent safe catamaran operation, the tour is rescheduled at no extra cost. Passenger safety is always the priority. You'll be notified in advance.
Are there parasailing restrictions?
Yes. Parasailing requires a minimum weight of 40 kg and maximum of 120 kg per person. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Ask us when booking if you have concerns.
What time does the catamaran depart?
Two schedules available: 9:30 AM (returns at 5:30 PM) and 11:30 AM (returns at 7:00 PM). Let us know your preference when booking.
How do I book?
Via WhatsApp — press the booking button, send your message and we reply in minutes.
Is the guide bilingual?
Yes, our local guides speak Spanish and English.
What does all-inclusive mean?
Open bar (with and without alcohol) throughout the trip, buffet lunch on the boat, snorkel gear and parasailing. No extra charges on the boat.
Where does the catamaran depart from?
Playa Langosta, Hotel Zone, Cancún. Two schedules: 9:30 AM (returns 5:30 PM) or 11:30 AM (returns 7 PM).

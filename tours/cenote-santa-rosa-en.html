About this tour

Two very different cenotes in one visit: • 1 open cenote with turquoise crystal-clear water — perfect for swimming and photos • 1 underground cenote more than 6 meters deep — a completely different and exciting experience The park also has recreational areas, bicycles to explore at your own pace, and Instagram-worthy photo spots throughout. This is the most accessible price point in our selection, without sacrificing the quality of the experience. A great starting point if it's your first time visiting a cenote. Meeting point: Homún main entrance, 1 block to the right.

About this tour

5 completely different cenotes in one tour: • 2 underground cenotes — a unique below-ground experience • 3 open cenotes, including 1 river-style — unlike any cenote you've seen Local bilingual guide, snorkel gear, life vests, parking and access to all common areas included. Cenotes Casa Tortuga is one of the most complete and varied cenote complexes in the Riviera Maya, located just outside Tulum. Unlike the more commercial cenotes in the area, the experience here is more intimate and authentic. Meeting point: Tulum, Quintana Roo, at the entrance of the town.

What is a river-style cenote?
It's an elongated cenote where the water flows gently through vegetation, similar to an underground river. At Casa Tortuga one of the open cenotes has this unique feature — perfect for floating peacefully through nature.

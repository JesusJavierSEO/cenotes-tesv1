Homún Cenotes: Yucatán's best-kept secret

The town of Homún, located just a short drive from Chichén Itzá, is home to more than 20 cenotes with crystal-clear waters that form part of the impressive underground cave network of the Yucatán Peninsula. Unlike the well-known cenotes of Tulum or Valladolid, Homún's cenotes remain quiet, authentic, and unspoiled.

If you're looking for cenotes near Chichén Itzá, Homún is the perfect complement to your visit to the archaeological site. Our cenote packages include a local guide and snorkel gear, so all you need to do is show up and enjoy.

The cenotes of Homún, Yucatán are ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and connecting with Mayan nature in an almost untouched setting. Join us and discover why travelers from around the world consider this one of the most memorable experiences of their trip to Mexico.

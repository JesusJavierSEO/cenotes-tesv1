About Isla Mujeres

A paradise 20 minutes from Cancún

Isla Mujeres is a small island just 20 minutes by ferry from Cancún. Turquoise waters, North Beach (consistently ranked among the world's best), and a relaxed vibe make it the perfect contrast to Cancún's bustle.

In the channel between Isla Mujeres and Cancún lies the MUSA (Underwater Museum of Art) — the world's first underwater sculpture museum, with 500+ submerged sculptures now colonized by living coral.

Our catamaran tour is the most comfortable and complete way to experience it all in one day.
